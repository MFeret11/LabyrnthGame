******************************README*******************************************
****************************How to Play*******************************

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move the ball through the maze.
The goal of the game is to get as far as you can in the time limit without hitting any red circles.
The ball speeds up the futher you get in the game.
Play again to beat your highscore!

****************************Known Bugs********************************
Level counter fails to reset. 
Ensure the aspect ratio is not set to Free Aspect. This can distort the layout of the UI elements.
